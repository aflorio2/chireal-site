Adrien Florio is the Principal Investigator of the QuIReal (Quantum Information and Real-time evolution in QFT) Emmy Noether research group at Bielefeld University.

## Research Interests

His research focuses on the intersection of quantum information theory and quantum field theory, with particular emphasis on:

- Quantum information in curved spacetime
- Real-time evolution in quantum field theory
- Quantum entanglement in high-energy physics
- Computational methods for quantum field theory
